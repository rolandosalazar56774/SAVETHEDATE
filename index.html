Our Story

How We Got Here

How We Met · 2019

She helped me with Calculus

We met in college — Lauren was kind enough to help Rolando with Calculus. One study session turned into long conversations, and somewhere in the middle of COVID, we found each other. The world stopped, but something between us started.

The Proposal · Spring Break

He couldn't wait another second

He had ordered the ring online and accidentally shipped it to his mom's house in Eagle Pass. So he drove to pick it up during spring break. When he walked back in and saw Lauren at her desk — working from home, not expecting a thing — he didn't even make it to the living room. He got down on one knee right there and asked her. He just couldn't wait.

Together since October 5, 2020 · Five years of beach vacations, good food, and better company.

Getting There

Travel Guide

Everything you need to plan your trip from Texas to paradise.

Fly into Honolulu International (HNL) — just 20 minutes from Waikiki by rideshare.

SAT ✈ HNL

San Antonio → Honolulu
1 stop via Dallas (DFW) or Houston (IAH)
Total travel time: ~10–13 hours
Estimated cost: $500–$900 round trip

Best airlines: American, United, Hawaiian Airlines
Book early: 6–9 months ahead for best fares

DFW ✈ HNL

Dallas → Honolulu
Often direct on American Airlines (~9 hours)
Estimated cost: $450–$800 round trip

Pro tip: DFW has more direct flight options to HNL — worth the drive from Eagle Pass.

Getting around Waikiki is easy — here's what to know.

Airport → Hotel
Uber/Lyft: ~$35–$50, 20–30 min
Taxi: ~$40–$60
Avoid rental cars unless you plan to explore the island

In Waikiki
Everything is walkable — beach, restaurants, shopping
The Bus: $3/ride, covers all of Oahu
Biki Bikes: fun way to cruise the waterfront

Day Trips
Diamond Head: short drive or rideshare
North Shore: ~1 hour drive, worth every minute

🌺 Book your hotel early
Waikiki fills up fast. Book 6+ months ahead.

💳 Budget planning
Budget $150–$200/day per person for food and activities beyond the hotel.

☀️ Pack smart
Reef-safe sunscreen, a light cover-up, and comfortable walking shoes.

⏰ Hawaii time
Hawaii is UTC-10 — 5 hours behind Texas (CST). Adjust before you fly.

🤙 The Shaka
Thumb and pinky out. It means aloha, hang loose, all good. You'll use it.

🐢 Respect the wildlife
Hawaiian green sea turtles rest on beaches. Stay 10 feet back — it's the law.
